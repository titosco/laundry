Questions & Answers
What if my cloths are heavily stained?

We try our best to remove the stains by adapting the best washing cycle, detergent, temperature suitable for the garment, however in case of tough stains, we will recommend you to use our stain removal or dry cleaning service.

How long would it take you to return the garments?

It takes us 4days to deliver your laundry come rain, come sunshine. For express service its just 24 hours and for ironing just 24 hours also.
